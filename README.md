Business Sales Performance Analytics Task 1 – Final Report
1. Objective
The objective of this project is to analyze business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-selling
products, high-value categories, and regional performance in order to provide actionable business
insights for decision-making.
2. Tools Used
Excel / Power BI / Tableau / Python were used for data cleaning, analysis, visualization, and insight
generation.
3. Data Cleaning & Preparation
• Removed missing and duplicate records
• Corrected data types for date and numeric columns
• Calculated revenue using Quantity × Price
4. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
• Total Revenue
• Monthly and Quarterly Sales Trends
• Top-Selling Products
• Category-wise Sales Performance
• Region-wise Sales Performance
• Average Order Value (AOV)
5. Key Insights
• Sales show a consistent growth trend over time.
• A small number of top-selling products generate the majority of revenue.
• Electronics and Fashion categories contribute high revenue.
• North and West regions perform better compared to other regions.
• Some products and regions require improvement due to low sales.
6. Dashboard Overview
An interactive dashboard was created including monthly revenue trend charts, top products bar charts,
category-wise pie charts, region-wise sales analysis, and KPI summary cards.
7. Recommendations
• Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products and regions.
• Introduce promotional offers for low-selling categories.
• Plan inventory based on high-demand products.
• Use seasonal trends to improve sales during low-performing periods.
8. Conclusion
This Business Sales Performance Analytics project provides meaningful insights into sales behavior,
customer demand, and regional performance. The analysis supports data-driven decisions that can
improve overall business profitability
